### The "3 sync points" issue (important for paper-writeup)
Adding a tracepoint to FISH currently requires synchronized changes in THREE separate places — easy to forget one and silently miss events:
1. **libtracetools.so registration** (install_fish_tracepoints tp_call.h/tracetools.h/.c)
2. **`tracetools_trace` names.py DEFAULT_EVENTS_ROS** (so `ros2 trace` API knows the event)
3. **`config/fish_events.txt`** (so FISH's own trace_session.sh injects it into the lttng enable-event whitelist)

Worth refactoring: make fish_events.txt the single source of truth, generate names.py and the trace_session.sh enable list from it.
